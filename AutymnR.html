About Me

I am a girl from Kent, Washington with roots in the Columbia River Basin farming region. Since I was little, I have loved drawing and painting. Creative ideas have always been a part of my family. I made it my mission to keep busy with a hobby, whether I was drawing, sewing, embroidering, or painting. This is a collection of some of my favorite pieces that I have done over the years. Enjoy!

Mini Me

A painting of a baby photo of myself. Personally, I believe that this is the best painting I have ever done. During previous projects I was afraid of doing people due to the difficulty. I approached drawing people with this saying, "When you start drawing you come across a crossroads, you draw people or anything else" and I choose everything else.

Completed (Sept 2022)

Herring Still Life

This oil painting stands as my largest painting to date at 30x40in. It was made in a span of a week with many late nights. The painting was made for my Aunt and Herring is her last name, so I changed the label on the bottle from Southern Comfort. The painting is forever held by my uncle's bar where it will forever be a great conversation piece.

Completed (Dec 2023)

Mountain Lupine

This is a painting of a photo that a family friend took. I created the painting for that friend’s birthday along with an embroidered jean jacket. The painting was the first to be made at college in a small dorm with little to no ventilation.

Completed (Dec 2023)

Drive

Origin: My project in class was based off the song, Drive by Alan Jackson thats about growing up driving many different things as you grow up with your parents. I chose to display some of my most important points in life through a concept that runs deep in my family.

  1. Boat on the Sound

    • Origin: It is my family's old boat with added memorable elements such as the captain’s hat, and Legos. My sisters and I used to fight over who wore the captain's hat, we used to play with the Lego’s all the time, etc.
    • Completed (April 2023)

  2. How Are Memories Made, Girls?

    • Origin: My family has a UTV that I learned how to drive with. One day I was driving back and forth on a beach, getting faster as I went, creating a massive dust cloud, creating my inspiration.
    • Completed (May 2023)

  3. To The Next Chapter

    • Origin: I made a painting showing the next step in my life at the time, college. I was leaving the rainforest of the coast to the desert of Eastern Washington.
    • Completed (June 2023)

Oil Painting 101

For my first oil painting class we had to do 4 paintings in 9 weeks along with other classes. We did an analogous, complementary, self-portrait and a masters copy painting. I got the chance to paint my favorite type of composition, still life’s.

Dancing

My sister was a part of Washington State University Country Swing Club and that inspired me to create a club of my own at Gonzaga. The video shows the shared experience of the importance of dancing in my family.
